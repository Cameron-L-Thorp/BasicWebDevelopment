Summation of Ghost Lore

Ghosts as an overall category are both fascinating and terrifying to the average person. There are many different types and temperaments within the broad category of ghosts, though all have their place in the supernatural realm and lore of various human cultures. It is often-times argued that the manner in which the living being passed to the spiritual plane dictates both if their soul becomes a ghost, and the methods they interact with their surroundings. Many cultures to this day have reverence for these souls, while others maintain a distinct fear, but the frequency legends and tales surrounding ghosts exist indicate there is more than meets the eye.

-Cameron Thorp 20220831

Summation of Pirates

Through the ages, there have been many groups of violent and unlawful individuals, and few are more notorious than pirates! Although there has been centuries of folktales and fictional stories, mudding the waters, surrounding these outlaws, few do justice to the true tales. From the betrayal and resurgence of Henry Avery, to the womanizing and terror of Edward Thatch, to the household name Henry Morgan, to the flamboyant and audacious Jack Rackham, the good the bad and the ugly characters who partook in this lifestyle simply cannot be captured in any single account. Some view them as truly free and equal, some see them as scoundrels and thieves, but most acknowledge they were globally pivotal in more than one historical period.

-Cameron Thorp 20220831
